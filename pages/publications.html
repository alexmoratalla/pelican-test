1. Phonons in single-layer and few-layer MoS2 and WS2, A. Molina-Sánchez and L. Wirtz, Physical Review B 84 155413 (2011).

In 2011 I started to investigate 2D Materials. My first work studied phonons in single-layer and bulk MoS2. Many times, properties of 2D materials behave in a counter-intuitive way with respect to their bulk counterparts. That is also the case of phonons in monolayer MoS2.
